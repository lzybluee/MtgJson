<Rulings>2008/05/01: <Counterbore> has no effect on cards on the battlefield that have the same name as the targeted spell.

2008/05/01: If the targeted spell can't be countered (it's Vexing Shusher, for example), that spell will remain on the stack. <Counterbore> will continue to resolve. You still get to search for and exile all other cards with that name.</Rulings>
